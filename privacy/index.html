1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Matthysen Holding BV ("we", "us") handles information when you use Orion: A Word Puzzle Game (the "App") on iOS. We have designed the App to collect no directly identifying information about you — no name, email, phone number, or password. The minimal data we do process is described below.

Matthysen Holding BV is the data controller for the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Dutch Implementation Act (UAVG).

2. Information we collect

What we don't collect

We do not ask for or store your name, email address, phone number, password, photo, contacts, or location. There is no sign-up form.

Anonymous account & gameplay progress

When you first open the App, an anonymous account identifier is generated and stored on your device. This identifier is used to save your gameplay progress to our database — level completions, achievements, in-game currency balances (Star Dust, Dream Dust), and purchase entitlements (e.g. Remove Ads). The identifier is a random string and is not linked to your real identity. Under GDPR, online identifiers can still constitute personal data, so we treat it as such.

Purchase information

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment card, Apple ID, or billing address. We receive only a product identifier and a receipt indicator (via RevenueCat) so we can grant the purchased item to your anonymous account.

Advertising

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. If you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission, AdMob may receive your Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) to serve personalised ads. If you decline, ads are non-personalised. We never receive your IDFA — it stays between your device and Google.

3. How we use information & legal basis

We process the limited data described above for the following purposes, on the following legal bases under Article 6 GDPR:

  • Provide the App and save your progress — Art. 6(1)(b), performance of the contract between you and us.
  • Deliver in-app purchases — Art. 6(1)(b), performance of the contract.
  • Serve non-personalised advertising — Art. 6(1)(f), legitimate interest in funding the App.
  • Serve personalised advertising (only if you opt in via ATT) — Art. 6(1)(a), your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

We do not use your data for profiling, scoring, or any decision with legal effect. We do not sell your data.

5. Children's privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has somehow provided us personal information, contact us and we will investigate and erase it.

6. Your rights & deleting your account

You can delete your account and all associated gameplay data at any time, directly from within the App: Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Under the GDPR you also have the right to: access the data we hold about you, request correction or erasure, restrict or object to processing, request data portability, and withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent). To exercise any of these rights, contact support@worldmgmt.xyz.

7. Data retention

Anonymous gameplay data is retained while your account exists. When you delete your account from the Profile screen, all associated data is erased immediately, except where retention is required by law (e.g. Apple's purchase records for tax compliance, which Apple — not we — control).

8. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest, and row-level access controls so each anonymous account can only access its own row. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect data continuously.

9. Business transfers

We may transfer the App and the data described in this policy to an affiliated company within our group (including a future transfer to World Management) or to a successor in the event of a reorganisation, merger, or sale of assets. Any successor will be bound by this Privacy Policy or a policy at least as protective. We will notify you in the App and on this page before any such transfer takes effect.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
